    How to use

    1. Select or drop files in the order you want them in the PDF.
    2. Adjust image quality if your PDF is too large or too soft.
    3. Click Create PDF and save the file when your browser prompts you.

    FAQs

    Will my Word document look identical?
    No—.docx is converted to plain text for reliability in the browser. For pixel-perfect layout, export PDF from Word or print to PDF.

    Are files uploaded?
    No. jsPDF and conversion run locally in your browser.

    PDF file size?
    Depends on image dimensions, quality slider, and number of pages.
