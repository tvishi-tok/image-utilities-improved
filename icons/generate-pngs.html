Icon PNG generator

Renders app-icon.svg into the PNG sizes the PWA manifest and browsers need. Click Generate & download and the four files save automatically.

It produces:

  • icon-192.png - Android home-screen / manifest
  • icon-512.png - splash screen, large surfaces
  • icon-maskable-512.png - Android adaptive icon (10 % safe-zone padding)
  • apple-touch-icon-180.png - iOS home-screen icon
or just click Generate (auto-fetches when served over HTTP)
